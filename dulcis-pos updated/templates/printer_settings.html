Quick Setup Guide

NETWORK PRINTER (Recommended)

1
Connect printer to router via LAN cable or Wi-Fi
2
On the printer: hold FEED button while powering on → prints IP address
3
Enter that IP here, keep port 9100
4
Click Save Settings then Test Print

CASH DRAWER WIRING

1
Cash drawer connects to the printer's RJ-12 port (not to the PC)
2
Enable "Cash Drawer Kick" toggle above
3
Drawer opens automatically on every cash sale
4
Manual open: press Open Drawer button on receipt screen

Compatible printers: Epson TM-T82X, TM-T88 · Star TSP100, TSP650 · Bixolon SRP-350 · any 80mm ESC/POS printer
